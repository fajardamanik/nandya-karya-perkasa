Our Journey

1980s

The Jakarta Foundation

Nandya Karya Perkasa began its roots in Jakarta, starting as a small-scale electrical components supplier focused on local industrial needs.

1993

PT. NKP Establishment

Official incorporation of PT. Nandya Karya Perkasa. We expanded our facility to include advanced cable extrusion lines to serve the growing national grid.

2000s

Reaching Global Markets

Achieved ISO certifications and began exporting to international markets. NKP became a trusted partner for global infrastructure projects across SE Asia and beyond.

Today

Innovation & Sustainability

Focusing on high-voltage technology and the #movingreen initiative, leading the transition to eco-friendly energy cabling.
